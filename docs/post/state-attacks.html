Nation-State Attacks 2025

Published: Mar 2025

Nation-state cyber operations have intensified in 2025, with coordinated attacks targeting critical infrastructure, healthcare systems, and AI research facilities. These campaigns often blend phishing, supply-chain compromises, and custom-built zero-days.

Attribution points to multiple advanced persistent threat (APT) groups aligned with regional geopolitical strategies. Organizations are urged to adopt Zero Trust models and improve monitoring of third-party vendors.
