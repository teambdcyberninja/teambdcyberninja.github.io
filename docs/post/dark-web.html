Dark Web Market Trends

Published: Feb 2025

Dark web marketplaces continue to evolve rapidly, with new players emerging while older ones are being shut down by law enforcement. Cryptocurrency mixers remain critical to laundering illicit funds, despite increased blockchain tracking.

Current trends include a rise in Ransomware-as-a-Service (RaaS), counterfeit identity documents, and bundled exploit kits targeting enterprise VPN systems.
